## UI/UX Design Principles

### Visual Hierarchy

**1. Evolution Events** (Highest Priority)
- **Large, animated notifications** for trait emergence
- **Haptic feedback** synchronized with visual cues
- **Poetic language** describing changes

**2. Creature Display** (Primary Content)
- **Real-time morphological changes** as traits evolve
- **Emergent names** appearing above creatures
- **Trail visualization** showing evolutionary history

**3. Environmental Context** (Supporting Information)  
- **Pollution visualization** with color overlays
- **Material affinity indicators** with magnetic field effects
- **Pack formation** highlights and connection lines

**4. System Information** (Background Data)
- **Generation counter** with progress indicators
- **Data export controls** for analysis
- **Debug tools** for development

### Animation Principles

**Organic Motion**: All animations feel **biological** not mechanical
- **Breathing rhythms** for idle states
- **Growth patterns** for trait emergence  
- **Flow dynamics** for material interactions
- **Pulse rhythms** for haptic synchronization

**Emergent Timing**: **No fixed durations** - animations **respond to evolution events**
- **Faster changes** during high mutation periods
- **Slower, contemplative** movements during stable periods
- **Dramatic flourishes** for significant evolutionary leaps

## Interaction Design Language

### Gesture Vocabulary

**Primary Gestures** (Core Interactions):
- **Tap**: Observe, focus attention
- **Long Press**: Deep observation, trait inspection
- **Swipe**: Movement, influence direction  
- **Pinch**: Scale, zoom, intensity control
- **Drag**: Material movement, trait transfer

**Secondary Gestures** (Advanced Interactions):
- **Double Tap**: Quick actions, shortcuts
- **Rotate**: Perspective shift, time manipulation
- **Multi-finger**: Complex environmental interactions

### Haptic Language

**Evolutionary Moments**:
- **Trait Emergence**: Rising pulse sequence (soft → strong)
- **Pack Formation**: Synchronized vibration harmony
- **Environmental Shock**: Sharp impact + decay tremor
- **Discovery**: Gentle exploration tap
- **Crisis**: Urgent heartbeat rhythm

**Interaction Feedback**:
- **Material Contact**: Magnetic snap sensation
- **Creature Approach**: Proximity pulse strengthening
- **Successful Influence**: Satisfying confirmation thump
- **Environmental Change**: Environmental resonance wave
